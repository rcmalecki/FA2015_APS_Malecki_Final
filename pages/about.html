Northern Moon Designs

Unique Handmade Jewelry & Accessories

Thank you for visiting Northern Moon Designs! My name is Rhonda, and I am the Minneapolis-based jewelry artist behind-the-scenes. Did you know that the art of jewelry-making is as old as the human race? Isn't it amazing to think that every time we adorn ourselves in this age-old tradition, we are connecting to this rich past? This one of the many reasons I love this craft. I specialize in making unique, handcrafted jewelry and accessories inspired by history, fashion, and the natural world. Seashells, glass, and semi-precious gemstones are my favorite materials to work with and I go to great lengths to locate quality supplies from all over the world.

To see what's currently for sale, please visit my Etsy store. Thanks, and I hope that you find something that you love there!
